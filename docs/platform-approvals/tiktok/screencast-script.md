# TikTok Content Posting API — Screencast Script

Target length: 90 seconds. Format: MP4 at 1920x1080.

## Storyboard + Voiceover

```
00:00–00:05  [Opening: WeFixTrades logo + "SocialSync — TikTok"]
             VO: "Hi, I'm Alex from WeFixTrades. SocialSync is our
                  done-for-you social posting service for trades
                  businesses. Today I'll show how it works with TikTok."

00:05–00:20  [Cut to admin dashboard. Show a sample client card
              "Northside Electrical."]
             VO: "Mike runs a small electrical shop. He's never posted
                  on TikTok but he knows his competitors are getting
                  jobs from it. He just signed up for SocialSync."

00:20–00:40  [Click into the portal → SocialSync → Connections tab →
              "Connect TikTok". Show the TikTok OAuth consent screen
              with the requested scopes clearly visible. Approve.]
             VO: "He clicks Connect TikTok. He sees the standard TikTok
                  consent screen listing the exact permissions —
                  identity, profile, and content publishing. He
                  approves with his business TikTok account."

00:40–01:00  [Cut to SocialSync content calendar showing a queued video
              draft: 'Quick tip: testing a GFCI outlet' with a 30-second
              clip and caption. Click "Approve & Post."]
             VO: "Our AI has generated a short tip video from the photos
                  and notes Mike submitted last week. He previews it,
                  reads the caption, and hits Approve and Post."

01:00–01:20  [Cut to the actual TikTok account in a phone simulator
              showing the video live with timestamp.]
             VO: "Within seconds the video is live on Mike's TikTok
                  account. Our backend used the Content Posting API
                  to upload and publish — Mike never opens the
                  TikTok app."

01:20–01:30  [Closing: privacy commitment frame.]
             VO: "Mike's TikTok tokens are encrypted at rest. He can
                  disconnect from his portal or from TikTok's app
                  permissions screen at any time. Thanks for reviewing."
```

## Production Notes

- TikTok reviewers explicitly want to see the OAuth consent screen mid-screencast — do not cut around it.
- Show the video metadata (caption, thumbnail) clearly so the reviewer can verify content quality.
- Filename: `wefixtrades-socialsync-tiktok-review.mp4`.
