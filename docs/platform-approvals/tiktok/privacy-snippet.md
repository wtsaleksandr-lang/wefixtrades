## TikTok

When you connect your TikTok account to WeFixTrades SocialSync, we use the TikTok Login Kit and the TikTok Content Posting API to publish video content on your behalf and to retrieve basic account metrics for reporting.

**What we access:**

- Your TikTok user ID, username, display name, and avatar (so we can show "Connected as @yourhandle" in your WFT portal).
- Your follower count and aggregate video statistics, so we can deliver the monthly performance report.

**What we publish:**

- Short-form videos that you have either explicitly approved in your WeFixTrades portal or that have been published under your SocialSync autopilot settings. Every video is created from photos, video clips, and job notes that you supplied to WeFixTrades.

**What we never do:**

- We never read your TikTok DMs, comments, or messages.
- We never enumerate or read other users' content via your account.
- We never share your TikTok access or refresh tokens with any third party. Tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM and are decrypted only inside our publishing worker.

**Your control:**

- Disconnect any time from the WeFixTrades portal (SocialSync → Connections → Disconnect TikTok) or directly from TikTok's app permission settings.
- On disconnect, your stored TikTok tokens are wiped within 24 hours and SocialSync stops publishing immediately.
- Email support@wefixtrades.com to request a full export or deletion of all SocialSync data associated with your account.

**Data retention:**

Encrypted TikTok tokens are retained only while your subscription is active plus a 30-day grace window. Aggregate video performance counts are retained for 24 months for reporting and then deleted.
