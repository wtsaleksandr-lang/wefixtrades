# Pinterest API Standard Access — Screencast Script

Target length: 75 seconds. 1920x1080 MP4.

## Storyboard + Voiceover

```
00:00–00:05  [Opening: WFT logo + "SocialSync — Pinterest"]
             VO: "Hi, I'm Alex from WeFixTrades. We help small trades
                  businesses post on social media without doing it
                  themselves. Here's our Pinterest integration."

00:05–00:20  [Cut to portal Connections tab. Click "Connect Pinterest".
              Pinterest OAuth consent screen appears showing requested
              scopes — pause for full visibility.]
             VO: "Sarah runs a kitchen remodeling business. She
                  connects her Pinterest Business account. The Pinterest
                  consent screen lists each scope — read and write
                  boards and pins, read account info — and she approves."

00:20–00:35  [Show board picker dropdown in WFT portal — Sarah selects
              'Kitchen Remodels' as the default board.]
             VO: "She picks her Kitchen Remodels board as the default
                  target. New pins will land there unless she changes
                  it for a specific project."

00:35–01:00  [Cut to SocialSync content calendar. Show a queued pin
              with a before/after kitchen photo, title 'Modern white
              shaker kitchen — Vancouver BC', and a link back to the
              business website. Click 'Approve and Pin'.]
             VO: "Our AI has produced a pin from photos Sarah submitted
                  last week. It's tuned for local discovery — title,
                  description, and link all set. She clicks Approve."

01:00–01:15  [Cut to actual Pinterest in a new tab showing the new pin
              live on the Kitchen Remodels board, with the link going
              to her website.]
             VO: "The pin is live on her board within seconds, with the
                  link driving discovery traffic back to her site."

01:15–01:30  [Closing privacy frame with disconnect option.]
             VO: "Tokens are encrypted at rest. Sarah can disconnect
                  from her portal or from Pinterest at any time.
                  Thanks for reviewing."
```
