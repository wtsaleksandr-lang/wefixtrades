## Pinterest

When you connect your Pinterest Business account to WeFixTrades SocialSync, we use the Pinterest API v5 to create pins on your behalf and to read basic account and pin metadata for setup and reporting.

**What we access:**

- Your Pinterest user ID, username, and account avatar (so we can confirm the right account is connected).
- The list of boards on your account, so you can choose where new pins should be created.
- Metadata of pins we previously created via SocialSync, so we can show their status in your activity log.

**What we publish:**

- Pins (image + title + description + link) on the board(s) you designate as SocialSync targets. Every pin originates from photos and notes you supplied to WeFixTrades and is either explicitly approved by you or shipped under your autopilot setting.

**What we never do:**

- We never read your saved pins, followed boards, search history, or any Pinterest activity outside of SocialSync's scope.
- We never share your Pinterest tokens with any third party. Tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM.

**Your control:**

- Disconnect at any time from your WeFixTrades portal (SocialSync → Connections → Disconnect Pinterest) or directly from Pinterest's app permissions page.
- On disconnect, your stored tokens are wiped within 24 hours.

**Data retention:**

Encrypted tokens are retained only while your subscription is active plus a 30-day grace window. Pin performance metrics are retained for 24 months for reporting.
