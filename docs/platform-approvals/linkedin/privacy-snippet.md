## LinkedIn

When you connect your LinkedIn account to WeFixTrades SocialSync, we use the LinkedIn Marketing Developer Platform APIs to publish posts on behalf of the LinkedIn Company Page (or personal profile) you authorize.

**What we access:**

- Your LinkedIn name, headline, and avatar (so we can show "Connected as ..." in your WFT portal).
- Your email address (only to confirm the authorized LinkedIn identity matches your WFT account).
- The list of LinkedIn Company Pages you administer (so you can pick which one SocialSync targets).
- Aggregate post metrics (impressions, reactions, comments) for the monthly SocialSync performance report.

**What we publish:**

- Text posts (with optional image link) on the LinkedIn Company Page or personal profile you designated. Every post originates from your own content and either receives your explicit approval or ships under your autopilot setting.

**What we never do:**

- We never read your LinkedIn messages, connections list, feed activity, or any data outside the SocialSync scopes you consented to.
- We never share your LinkedIn tokens with any third party. Tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM.

**Your control:**

- Disconnect at any time from your WFT portal (SocialSync → Connections → Disconnect LinkedIn) or from https://www.linkedin.com/psettings/permitted-services.
- On disconnect, your stored LinkedIn tokens are wiped within 24 hours.

**Data retention:**

Encrypted tokens are retained only while your subscription is active plus a 30-day grace window. Aggregate post performance metrics are retained for 24 months for reporting.
