## Facebook and Instagram (Meta)

When you connect your Facebook Page and/or linked Instagram Business account to WeFixTrades SocialSync, we use Meta's Graph API to publish content on your behalf and to retrieve performance metrics for reporting.

**What we access:**

- The list of Facebook Pages you manage, so you can choose which page SocialSync posts to.
- The selected page's posts and engagement metrics, so we can avoid duplicate content and report monthly reach and reactions.
- The Instagram Business account linked to your selected Facebook Page (only if you choose to enable Instagram cross-posting).

**What we publish:**

- AI-generated weekly posts (text + optional image) that you have either explicitly approved or enabled to ship via autopilot.

**What we never do:**

- We never read or store your personal Facebook or Instagram timeline, friends list, photos, or messages.
- We never post to your personal Facebook profile — only to the Pages you explicitly connect.
- We never share your Meta access tokens with any third party. Tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM and decrypted only inside our publishing worker.

**Your control:**

- You can disconnect at any time from your WeFixTrades portal (SocialSync → Connections → Disconnect) or directly from Facebook Business Settings → Business Integrations.
- On disconnect, your stored access token is wiped within 24 hours and SocialSync stops all publishing for that account immediately.
- You can request deletion of all SocialSync data associated with your account by emailing support@wefixtrades.com or via the in-portal "Delete my data" button.

**Data retention:**

Encrypted access tokens are retained only for the duration of your active subscription plus a 30-day grace window. Aggregate post performance counts (impressions, reactions) are retained for 24 months to support year-over-year reporting and then deleted.
