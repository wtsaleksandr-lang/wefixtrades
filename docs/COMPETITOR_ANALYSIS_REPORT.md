## 7. GAPS -- What Competitors Do NOT Offer

### GAP 1: Done-For-You Review Responses (Not Just AI Templates)
**Every single competitor** offers either: (a) AI-generated response suggestions you still have to approve/send, or (b) basic template responses. NONE offer a service where a real human crafts personalized responses on behalf of the business. For a busy plumber who finishes a job at 8pm, even clicking "approve" on an AI suggestion is friction. A true done-for-you response service where reviews are responded to by a human team within hours -- without the tradesperson lifting a finger -- does not exist in this space.

### GAP 2: Trade-Specific Positioning
**Zero of 8 competitors** position specifically for tradespeople. They all say "local business." NiceJob integrates with Jobber and Housecall Pro (trade tools), and Broadly has 22% construction users, but NEITHER puts "plumber," "electrician," or "HVAC" in their headline or primary positioning. A tradesperson visiting any of these sites has to mentally translate generic "local business" language to their world. There is no "Built for Tradespeople" player.

### GAP 3: Simplicity for Non-Tech-Savvy Users
All 8 competitors are self-service SaaS platforms that require:
- Creating an account
- Configuring settings
- Connecting review platforms
- Setting up automated workflows
- Managing a dashboard
- Approving/editing AI responses

For a solo electrician who barely uses email, this is overwhelming. NONE offer a "we set everything up for you, just give us your business name" experience. The onboarding is always self-serve.

### GAP 4: Truly Affordable Pricing for Solo Tradespeople
The cheapest option is ReputationStacker at $69/mo. For a solo plumber doing $5-8K/month in revenue, that is $828/year for review management alone. Combined with other software costs (accounting, scheduling, etc.), this adds up fast. There is NO option under $50/mo that covers the basics well. And none offer a "pay per review" or usage-based model that scales with a small operator's needs.

### GAP 5: No-Contract Flexibility (Partially Addressed)
NiceJob and ReputationStacker offer month-to-month. But Birdeye (12-month lock-in), Podium (long-term contracts), and others still require commitments. The complaints about Birdeye and Podium cancellation difficulties are severe -- BBB complaints, Trustpilot horror stories. There is opportunity in loudly marketing "no contract ever, cancel in one click."

### GAP 6: Bundled Value
NONE bundle review management with other services tradespeople need:
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Before/after photo management
- Quote follow-up automation
- Job completion to review request automation
- Simple website/landing page
- Social media posting of completed jobs

They all sell review management as a standalone product. A tradesperson needs 4-5 separate tools to manage their online presence. Bundling review management with even one or two other services (like GBP optimization + simple website) at an affordable price would be unique.

### GAP 7: Proactive Reputation Protection
All competitors are REACTIVE -- they help you get reviews and respond to them. NONE proactively:
- Coach tradespeople on how to ask for reviews in person
- Provide scripts for the moment of service completion
- Offer negative review interception (catching unhappy customers before they leave a public review)
- Provide reputation recovery playbooks

### GAP 8: Outcome Guarantees
NONE guarantee outcomes. No "get 20 new reviews in 60 days or your money back." No "improve your rating by 0.5 stars guaranteed." In a market where tradespeople are skeptical of software promises, a simple outcome guarantee would be a powerful differentiator.

## SUMMARY: The Competitive Landscape in One View

**The market is splitting into two lanes:**
1. **Expensive all-in-one platforms** (Birdeye $299+, Podium $399+) that do everything but cost too much and lock you into contracts
2. **Affordable focused tools** (NiceJob $75, ReputationStacker $69, Broadly $79) that handle reviews but are still self-serve DIY software

**What does NOT exist:**
- A done-for-you review management SERVICE (not software) priced for solo tradespeople
- A platform that speaks directly to trades (plumbers, electricians, HVAC) in its positioning
- A truly simple, "we handle everything" experience that requires zero tech skill
- An affordable bundle that combines review management with other online presence needs
- Outcome guarantees that reduce purchase risk
- Human-crafted review responses (not AI templates)

**The WeFixTrades opportunity sits in the gap between "expensive enterprise software" and "cheap DIY tools" -- a done-for-you, trade-specific, affordable review management SERVICE that requires zero effort from the tradesperson.**
