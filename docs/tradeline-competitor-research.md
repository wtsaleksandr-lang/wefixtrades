## 2. Pricing Model Comparison

### Flat Monthly vs Per-Minute vs Hybrid

| Model | Providers | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|-------|-----------|------|------|----------|
| **Flat monthly** | Goodcall, VoiceCharm, Allo, ServiceAgent | Predictable cost, easy to sell | Provider eats cost on high-volume clients | Trades businesses (hate surprise bills) |
| **Per-minute** | Ruby, AnswerConnect, MAP, Vapi | Fair usage billing | Unpredictable bills, penalizes busy months | Agencies, low-volume businesses |
| **Per-call** | Smith.ai, Upfirst | Simple to understand | Expensive for long calls | Professional services (legal, medical) |
| **Hybrid (flat + overage)** | Dialzara, Rosie, Synthflow, Abby Connect | Predictable base + scales | Overage surprises possible | Growing small businesses |
| **Per unique customer** | Goodcall | Aligns cost with value | Unusual, harder to compare | Service businesses with repeat callers |

### What Trades Businesses Actually Prefer

Trades businesses strongly prefer **flat monthly pricing** because:
- They hate variable bills (already deal with material cost fluctuations)
- Call volume spikes during emergencies (HVAC in summer, plumbing in winter)
- Want to know exactly what they're paying before signing up
- VoiceCharm ($299/mo flat) and Goodcall (unlimited minutes) specifically target this preference

### B. Recommended Pricing Model: FLAT MONTHLY

**Why flat monthly wins for TradeLine™:**
- Trades businesses hate variable/per-minute billing
- Flat fee = easy to sell ("$197/mo, done")
- Predictable revenue for WeFixTrades
- Differentiates from Ruby/AnswerConnect per-minute billing
- Matches what VoiceCharm ($299/mo) and Goodcall (flat) already proved works

**Built-in protection:** Cap calls at tier limits (100/300/unlimited). If a Starter client consistently exceeds 100 calls, nudge upgrade to Professional.

### Where Margins Can Break

| Risk | Trigger | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|---------|--------|------------|
| High call volume | >600 min/mo on Starter | Cost jumps to $60+, margin drops to 38% | Enforce tier limits, auto-upgrade nudge |
| Long calls | Avg call >3 min (chatty callers) | LLM + TTS costs spike | Set max call duration (5 min), transfer to voicemail |
| LLM model cost increase | OpenAI/Anthropic price hike | Per-minute cost rises | Use smaller models (GPT-4.1 mini, Haiku) |
| ElevenLabs TTS | Premium voice at $0.10/min | 3x TTS cost vs Deepgram | Use Deepgram TTS ($0.011/min) for standard, ElevenLabs only for Premium tier |
| SMS abuse | Client gets 1,000+ spam texts | SMS costs spike | Rate-limit SMS, spam filtering |

## 8. Competitive Positioning

### How TradeLine™ Should Be Positioned

**NOT this:** "AI virtual receptionist" (generic, crowded, sounds like tech)
**NOT this:** "AI answering service" (commodity positioning, race to bottom)

**THIS:** "Your after-hours office team that never clocks out"

### Positioning Statement

> TradeLine™ is the 24/7 answering & lead capture system built for trades businesses. It answers calls, replies to missed calls, responds to messages across SMS, chat, and social — and books jobs while you're on the tools.

### Key Differentiators vs Competitors

| vs Competitor | TradeLine™ Advantage |
|---------------|---------------------|
| vs Rosie, Dialzara, Upfirst | Multi-channel (not just phone — SMS, chat, DMs) |
| vs VoiceCharm | Lower price ($197 vs $299), includes chat + DMs |
| vs Ruby, AnswerConnect | 5–10x cheaper, no per-minute billing |
| vs Goodcall | Includes SMS auto-reply + website chat (Goodcall is phone-only) |
| vs Smith.ai | Flat pricing (no per-call surprise bills) |
| vs generic AI tools | Built specifically for trades — plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing language |

### Messaging Angles That Work

1. **"Never miss a job again"** — speaks to the #1 pain (missed calls = lost revenue)
2. **"Your $197/mo office manager"** — compares to hiring ($3,500+/mo receptionist)
3. **"Works nights, weekends, holidays"** — trades businesses get emergency calls 24/7
4. **"Answers calls, texts, chats, and DMs — one system"** — multi-channel is the real differentiator
5. **"Set up in 10 minutes, not 10 days"** — speed to value matters for busy tradespeople
