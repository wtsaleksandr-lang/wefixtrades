### 1. SCORPION

- **URL:** https://www.scorpion.co
- **Target Audience:** Home services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing), legal, medical
- **Service Type:** Full-service agency (done-for-you) with proprietary platform
- **Pricing Model:** Custom pricing, 12-month contracts required
- **Apples-to-Apples:** PARTIAL — Full-service agency bundling SEO with web, ads, and reputation. Not SEO-only. Far higher price point.
- **Primary Promise:** "Get more jobs. Grow your business."

**Pricing**

| Tier | Price | Model |
|------|-------|-------|
| Home Services Package | $3,000–$10,000+/mo | Custom, 12-month contract |
| Website Build | ~$200/mo bundled (proprietary CMS) | Included in contract |
| Ad Spend | Additional | Client-funded |

**What's Included**

- SEO: YES — keyword research, on-page optimization, content, local SEO
- Website: YES — built on proprietary CMS (you do NOT own it)
- PPC/Ads: YES — Google Ads, LSA management
- Reputation: YES — review generation and management (Reputation AI)
- Reporting: YES — proprietary RevenueMAX dashboard
- Content: YES — blog posts, service pages
- Backlinks: Not explicitly featured
- Ongoing vs one-time: Ongoing only (12-month minimum)

**Proprietary Tools**

- RevenueMAX: AI marketing suite, campaign dashboard
- Ranking AI: Identifies high-revenue keywords, adapts strategy
- Scorpion Connect: AI customer service rep (answers, books, pays)
- Capacity Marketing Engine (2025): Integrates with ServiceTitan, adjusts ad spend based on availability

**Real Copy**

- **Headline:** "HVAC Marketing & Advertising Services"
- **CTA:** "Get a free consultation"
- **Repeated phrases:** "Book more jobs," "grow your business," "AI-powered"

**Trust Elements**

- 1,000+ employees
- Serves thousands of home services businesses
- ServiceTitan and Housecall Pro integrations
- Proprietary AI tools

**Key Weaknesses**

- You don't own your website — built on Scorpion's proprietary CMS, can't take it when you leave
- 12-month contracts with expensive exit clauses (reports of $24,000 buyout)
- Overworked account managers (dozens of accounts each)
- High minimum spend ($3,000+/mo) excludes small trades businesses
- Lead quality complaints despite high volume
- Aggressive upselling after initial commitment
