# QuoteQuick Pro™ — Competitor Research Dataset

## 1. Competitor Landscape (12 Companies)

Competitors fall into two distinct categories:
- **Category A**: Field service platforms with quoting as a feature (bundled)
- **Category B**: Standalone quote calculator / widget builders (embeddable)

## 2. Pricing Patterns

### By Category

| Segment | Price Range | Quoting Capability |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Standalone widget builders** | Free – $29/mo | Embeddable calculator, lead capture, basic formulas |
| **Budget field service platforms** | $30–$75/mo | Basic quote builder, templates, e-signatures |
| **Mid-market field service** | $119–$299/mo | Automated follow-ups, quote-to-job conversion, CRM |
| **Premium field service** | $250–$1,200+/mo | Flat-rate pricebooks, AI estimates, financing, on-site tools |
| **Trade-specific instant quote** | Custom (likely $200–$500+/mo) | Auto-calculated quotes from property data, rebates, financing |

### Cheapest vs Mid vs Premium

| Tier | Examples | Price | What You Get |

| **Cheapest** | Calconic, Elfsight, Markate | $5–$40/mo | Basic calculator widget OR basic field service with simple quoting |
| **Mid** | involve.me, ActiveCalculator, Jobber Core, QuoteIQ Essentials | $29–$119/mo | No-code calculator builder with templates + lead capture, OR field service with quote templates |
| **Premium** | Housecall Pro MAX, Jobber Grow, QuoteIQ Pro, Projul | $149–$399/mo | Full business platform with automated quoting, follow-ups, pricebooks |
| **Enterprise** | ServiceTitan, EDEN | $1,000–$5,000+/mo | AI-powered estimating, property data integration, financing, pricebook management |

## 3. Key Patterns Identified

### Bundled vs Standalone

| Pattern | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Most quoting tools are bundled** | 6 of 12 competitors bundle quoting inside a larger field service platform. You can't buy just the quoting feature. |
| **Standalone calculators exist but are generic** | ConvertCalculator, involve.me, Calconic, Elfsight, ActiveCalculator are general-purpose — not built for trades. They work for any industry. |
| **Only 2 competitors offer trade-specific instant quoting** | QuoteIQ (InstaQuote — customer self-quoting) and EDEN (HVAC instant quotes on website). This is a thin competitive space. |
| **No one offers an affordable, trades-specific, embeddable quote widget** | This is the gap. Field service platforms are $100+/mo and require full platform adoption. Generic widgets are cheap but not trades-aware. |

### How Quoting Is Typically Positioned

| Positioning | Companies |
|------------|-----------|
| "Close more jobs faster" | Jobber, Housecall Pro, QuoteIQ |
| "Professional quotes in minutes" | Markate, Jobber |
| "Let customers quote themselves" | QuoteIQ (InstaQuote), ConvertCalculator, involve.me |
| "Instant pricing transparency" | EDEN |
| "Lead generation tool" | involve.me, Elfsight, Stylish Cost Calculator, ActiveCalculator |
| "AI-powered estimating" | QuoteIQ, ActiveCalculator, ServiceTitan |
