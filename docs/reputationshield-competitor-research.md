## 2. Pricing Patterns

### By Tier

| Tier | Price Range | What You Get |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| **Budget** | $69–$99/mo | Automated review requests, basic monitoring (Google + 1–2 platforms), email/SMS campaigns, simple dashboard |
| **Mid-market** | $125–$349/mo | AI review responses, multi-platform monitoring, review campaigns, SMS included, mobile app, integrations |
| **Premium** | $399–$999/mo | Full suite: AI concierge, webchat, payments, advanced analytics, multi-location, listings management |
| **Enterprise** | $1,000–$5,000+/mo | 250+ site monitoring, custom surveys, proprietary scoring, dedicated support, API access |

### By Pricing Model

| Model | Companies | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| **Flat monthly** | NiceJob, ReputationStacker, Broadly (reviews tier) | Simple, predictable. Best for single-location trades businesses |
| **Per-location monthly** | Birdeye, ReviewTrackers, GatherUp, ReviewGrower | Scales with business size. Volume discounts at 4+ locations |
| **Per-seat (agency)** | Grade.us | Designed for resellers managing multiple clients |
| **Custom quote** | Podium, Reputation.com, Thryv | Enterprise/sales-driven. Opaque pricing |

## 5. How Agencies Package Reputation Services

| Packaging Pattern | Examples | Notes |
|------------------|----------|-------|
| **Review management as standalone SaaS** | Birdeye, GatherUp, Grade.us, ReputationStacker | Customer self-serves. Platform does the work |
| **Reviews bundled into all-in-one platform** | Thryv, Broadly, Podium | Reviews are one module among CRM, payments, messaging, scheduling |
| **Reviews as part of marketing retainer** | Agencies using Grade.us or GatherUp white-label | Agency charges $200–$500/mo, uses $40–$99 tool underneath. 60–80% margin |
| **Trades-specific review automation** | NiceJob | Integrates with Jobber/Housecall Pro to auto-request reviews after job completion |
| **Enterprise reputation intelligence** | Reputation.com, ReviewTrackers | Data/analytics focus, proprietary scoring, 250+ sites monitored |

## 6. Key Market Observations

| Observation | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **NiceJob is the only trades-specific player** | Integrates with Jobber, Housecall Pro, FieldPulse — auto-triggers review requests after jobs complete. $75–$125/mo |
| **AI review responses are the new differentiator** | Birdeye, Podium (Pro), Broadly (Pro) all added AI response writing in 2025–2026. Becoming table stakes |
| **Negative review filtering is underused** | Only Birdeye, Grade.us, and ReputationStacker route unhappy customers to private feedback instead of public reviews |
| **White-label is standard for agencies** | Grade.us ($40/seat) and ReviewGrower ($9.95/location) are the cheapest white-label options |
| **Most tools are SaaS self-serve** | Very few offer done-for-you review response writing. This is usually manual or AI-assisted by the business owner |
| **Per-location pricing dominates** | Most platforms charge $69–$349/mo per location, with volume discounts at 4+ locations |
| **Review generation + response is the core value** | Monitoring alone is not enough to charge premium. The value is in generating MORE reviews and responding professionally |
