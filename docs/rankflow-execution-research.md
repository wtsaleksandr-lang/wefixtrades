# A. TASK CATALOG

## 1. CONTENT TASKS

### 1.1 Service Page Creation
- **Description:** Write a 1,000–1,500 word SEO-optimized page targeting a specific service + location (e.g., "Emergency Plumber in Brampton"). Includes H1/H2 structure, keyword placement, internal links, CTA sections.
- **Who can do it:** AI (first draft) + freelancer (polish/localize) — or freelancer alone
- **Average cost:** AI: $0.50–$2/page (Claude API) | Freelancer: $50–$150/page | Agency: $200–$500/page
- **Turnaround:** AI: minutes | Freelancer: 2–5 days | Agency: 5–10 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** LOW — template-driven (trade + service + city = page)
- **Required access:** WordPress/CMS admin or FTP
- **Risk level:** LOW — worst case is thin content that doesn't rank

### 1.2 Blog Post Creation
- **Description:** Write a 800–1,200 word blog post targeting long-tail keywords (e.g., "How to prevent frozen pipes in winter"). Includes keyword targeting, internal links to service pages, images.
- **Who can do it:** AI (first draft) + human review — or freelancer alone
- **Average cost:** AI: $0.50–$2/post | Freelancer: $50–$150/post | Agency: $150–$300/post
- **Turnaround:** AI: minutes | Freelancer: 2–5 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** LOW — content briefs are templatable by trade
- **Required access:** WordPress/CMS admin
- **Risk level:** LOW

### 1.3 Local Landing Page Creation
- **Description:** Create location-specific landing pages targeting "[service] in [city/neighborhood]". 500–800 words with local signals (address, service area map, local reviews, NAP).
- **Who can do it:** AI (draft) + freelancer (localize) — highly templatable
- **Average cost:** AI: $0.50–$1/page | Freelancer: $30–$80/page | Agency: $100–$250/page
- **Turnaround:** AI: minutes | Freelancer: 1–3 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** VERY LOW — most templatable task (swap city name, localize stats)
- **Required access:** WordPress/CMS admin
- **Risk level:** LOW — caution needed to avoid doorway page penalties (must add unique local content)

## 2. ON-PAGE SEO TASKS

### 2.1 Title Tag & Meta Description Optimization
- **Description:** Audit and rewrite title tags and meta descriptions for all key pages. Target format: "[Service] in [City] | [Business Name]". Meta descriptions with CTA and keyword.
- **Who can do it:** AI (generate recommendations) + internal (apply) — or freelancer
- **Average cost:** AI: near-zero | Freelancer: $5–$15/page | Agency: $25–$50/page
- **Turnaround:** AI: instant | Freelancer: 1–2 days for full site
- **Difficulty to standardize:** VERY LOW — formula-based
- **Required access:** WordPress/CMS admin or Yoast/RankMath plugin
- **Risk level:** LOW

### 2.2 Header Structure & Keyword Optimization
- **Description:** Audit H1/H2/H3 structure on key pages. Ensure one H1 per page with primary keyword, H2s for sections with secondary keywords. Add missing keyword variations.
- **Who can do it:** AI (audit + recommendations) + freelancer (implement)
- **Average cost:** AI: near-zero | Freelancer: $10–$25/page | Agency: $50–$100/page
- **Turnaround:** 1–3 days for full site
- **Difficulty to standardize:** LOW — checklist-driven
- **Required access:** WordPress/CMS admin
- **Risk level:** LOW

### 2.3 Internal Linking Optimization
- **Description:** Add contextual internal links between service pages, blog posts, and location pages. Create a hub-and-spoke structure around core services.
- **Who can do it:** AI (suggest link map) + freelancer (implement)
- **Average cost:** Freelancer: $50–$150/site | Agency: $200–$500
- **Turnaround:** 2–5 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** MEDIUM — requires understanding site structure
- **Required access:** WordPress/CMS admin
- **Risk level:** LOW

### 2.4 Image Alt Text & Optimization
- **Description:** Add keyword-rich alt text to all images. Compress images for page speed. Add descriptive filenames.
- **Who can do it:** AI (generate alt text) + freelancer (apply + compress)
- **Average cost:** AI: near-zero | Freelancer: $25–$75/site
- **Turnaround:** 1–2 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** VERY LOW — bulk operation
- **Required access:** WordPress/CMS admin
- **Risk level:** LOW

## 3. TECHNICAL TASKS (Light)

### 3.1 LocalBusiness Schema Markup
- **Description:** Add JSON-LD structured data for LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, and Review schemas. Validate with Google Rich Results Test.
- **Who can do it:** AI (generate JSON-LD) + freelancer (inject into site)
- **Average cost:** AI: near-zero to generate | Freelancer: $25–$75 | Agency: $100–$300
- **Turnaround:** 1–2 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** LOW — template JSON-LD per trade type
- **Required access:** WordPress theme/plugin or header injection
- **Risk level:** LOW — invalid schema is ignored, not penalized

### 3.2 Page Speed Quick Fixes
- **Description:** Compress images, enable lazy loading, minify CSS/JS, enable browser caching. NOT deep code refactoring.
- **Who can do it:** Freelancer (WordPress plugin setup) — Autoptimize, ShortPixel, WP Rocket
- **Average cost:** Freelancer: $50–$200 | Agency: $200–$500
- **Turnaround:** 1–3 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** MEDIUM — depends on hosting/CMS/theme
- **Required access:** WordPress admin + hosting (sometimes FTP/SSH)
- **Risk level:** MEDIUM — plugin conflicts can break sites

### 3.3 XML Sitemap & Robots.txt Setup
- **Description:** Ensure XML sitemap is generated, submitted to Google Search Console, and robots.txt allows proper crawling. Fix any crawl errors.
- **Who can do it:** AI (audit) + freelancer (fix)
- **Average cost:** Freelancer: $25–$50 | Agency: $50–$150
- **Turnaround:** 1 day
- **Difficulty to standardize:** LOW — standard checklist
- **Required access:** WordPress admin + Google Search Console
- **Risk level:** LOW (HIGH if robots.txt blocks crawling — must verify)

### 3.4 Google Search Console Setup & Monitoring
- **Description:** Connect site to GSC, submit sitemap, monitor for errors, track impressions/clicks/positions. Flag issues monthly.
- **Who can do it:** Internal (setup) + AI (monitoring/reporting)
- **Average cost:** Near-zero (tool cost only)
- **Turnaround:** Setup: 1 day | Monitoring: ongoing/automated
- **Difficulty to standardize:** VERY LOW
- **Required access:** Google account + site verification
- **Risk level:** LOW

## 4. LOCAL SEO TASKS

### 4.1 Google Business Profile Optimization
- **Description:** Complete all GBP fields: categories, services, service areas, hours, description with keywords, photos, Q&A, posts. Already handled by MapGuard — RankFlow should leverage, not duplicate.
- **Who can do it:** Internal / MapGuard service
- **Average cost:** Already included in MapGuard | Standalone: $100–$397
- **Turnaround:** 2–5 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** LOW — checklist
- **Required access:** Google Business Profile admin
- **Risk level:** LOW

### 4.2 Citation Building (Top 50 Directories)
- **Description:** Submit business to top 50 local directories (Yelp, Yellow Pages, BBB, Bing Places, Apple Maps, etc.). Ensure consistent NAP across all.
- **Who can do it:** Freelancer or citation service (BrightLocal, Whitespark, Yext)
- **Average cost:** Freelancer: $2–$5/citation ($100–$250 for 50) | Service: $200–$500 one-time
- **Turnaround:** 2–4 weeks (directories take time to process)
- **Difficulty to standardize:** VERY LOW — same list for every client
- **Required access:** Business name, address, phone, website, category
- **Risk level:** LOW

### 4.3 NAP Consistency Audit & Cleanup
- **Description:** Scan existing citations for inconsistent Name, Address, Phone data. Fix mismatches. Remove duplicate listings.
- **Who can do it:** Tool (BrightLocal/Whitespark audit) + freelancer (cleanup)
- **Average cost:** Tool: $30–$50/audit | Freelancer cleanup: $50–$150
- **Turnaround:** Audit: instant | Cleanup: 1–2 weeks
- **Difficulty to standardize:** LOW — tool-driven
- **Required access:** Business details
- **Risk level:** LOW

### 4.4 Review Generation Strategy Setup
- **Description:** Set up review request email/SMS sequence. Create review links. Already handled by ReputationShield — RankFlow should cross-sell, not duplicate.
- **Who can do it:** Internal / ReputationShield service
- **Average cost:** Already included in ReputationShield
- **Turnaround:** 1–2 days setup
- **Difficulty to standardize:** LOW
- **Required access:** Client email list or CRM
- **Risk level:** LOW

## 5. OFF-PAGE TASKS

### 5.1 Local Directory Submissions (Beyond Top 50)
- **Description:** Submit to niche-specific directories (HomeAdvisor, Angi, Thumbtack, trade associations, local chambers of commerce).
- **Who can do it:** Freelancer
- **Average cost:** Freelancer: $3–$10/directory | Batch: $50–$150 for 20 directories
- **Turnaround:** 1–3 weeks
- **Difficulty to standardize:** MEDIUM — varies by trade and location
- **Required access:** Business details, sometimes account creation
- **Risk level:** LOW

### 5.2 Basic Link Building (Guest Posts / Local Links)
- **Description:** Acquire 2–5 backlinks/month from local blogs, trade directories, supplier pages, community sites, sponsorship pages.
- **Who can do it:** Freelancer (outreach) or link building service
- **Average cost:** Freelancer: $50–$200/link | Service: $100–$500/link | Bulk service: $300–$1,000/mo for 3–5 links
- **Turnaround:** 2–4 weeks per batch
- **Difficulty to standardize:** HIGH — requires outreach, negotiation, quality control
- **Required access:** None (external)
- **Risk level:** MEDIUM — low-quality links can harm rankings (must vet quality)

### 5.3 Social Profile Creation & Optimization
- **Description:** Create or optimize business profiles on Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter with consistent NAP, keywords, and links. One-time setup task.
- **Who can do it:** Freelancer or internal
- **Average cost:** Freelancer: $25–$75 | Internal: near-zero
- **Turnaround:** 1–2 days
- **Difficulty to standardize:** VERY LOW — checklist
- **Required access:** Social media account credentials
- **Risk level:** LOW

# E. COST STRUCTURE SUMMARY

| Tier | Revenue | Est. Delivery Cost | Est. Gross Margin | Margin % |
|------|---------|-------------------|-------------------|----------|
| Starter ($349) | $349/mo | $80–$130/mo | $220–$270/mo | **63–77%** |
| Growth ($599) | $599/mo | $225–$370/mo | $230–$375/mo | **38–63%** |
| Pro ($899) | $899/mo | $480–$730/mo | $170–$420/mo | **19–47%** |

**Key cost drivers:**
1. **Content creation** — $20–$50/page with AI + freelancer review (vs $150–$300 at agencies)
2. **Link building** — most variable cost. Directory submissions cheap ($3–$10/link), quality guest posts expensive ($100–$500/link)
3. **AI automation** — ranking reports, keyword research, content drafts, schema generation = near-zero marginal cost
4. **Recurring vs. setup** — Month 1 costs ~2x ongoing due to audits, citations, initial optimization. Margins improve from Month 2+.

**Margin improvement levers:**
- AI-first content (reduce freelancer dependency)
- Batch citation services (bulk pricing)
- Automated reporting (zero marginal cost)
- Template library (reduce per-client customization)
- Cross-sell MapGuard/ReputationShield (reduce local SEO task duplication)
