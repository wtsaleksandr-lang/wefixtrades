# SocialSync™ — Competitor Research Dataset

## 1. Competitor Landscape (14 Companies)

Competitors fall into four categories:
- **Category A**: Done-for-you social media services (managed, content created for you)
- **Category B**: Self-serve scheduling & management tools (SaaS — you create content)
- **Category C**: Full-service agencies (social media as part of broader marketing)
- **Category D**: AI-powered auto-posting services

## 2. Pricing Patterns

### By Service Type

| Service Type | Price Range | Posts/Month | Content Created By | Best For |
|-------------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|----------|
| **AI auto-posting** | $49/mo | 12 posts | AI (fully automated) | Businesses wanting zero effort |
| **Budget done-for-you** | $99–$249/mo | 12–full month | Human team | Small businesses wanting hands-off |
| **Self-serve scheduling tools** | $5–$249/mo | Unlimited (user creates) | Business owner / team | Businesses with someone who can create content |
| **Agency white-label fulfillment** | $149–$750/mo per client | 40–150 posts | Dedicated manager | Agencies reselling social media |
| **Mid-market agency** | $750–$1,550/mo | Varies (strategy + execution) | Agency team | Growing businesses wanting strategy + content |
| **Premium agency** | $3,000–$5,100/mo | 10–30 posts | Full creative team | Established businesses, enterprise |

### Low-End vs Mid vs Premium

| Tier | Price | Posts/Month | What You Get |
|------|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Low-end** | $49–$99/mo | 12 posts | AI or basic human content, 1–2 platforms, no strategy, no ads |
| **Budget DFY** | $199–$249/mo | 12 posts (3/week) | Human-created content, FB + IG, trades-specific (Footbridge), no strategy |
| **Mid-market** | $750–$1,550/mo | 15–30+ posts | Strategy, custom content, account manager, reporting, 2–3 platforms |
| **Premium** | $3,000–$5,100/mo | 10–30 posts | Full creative team, competitive analysis, boosted posts, 1–3 networks, monthly consultations |

## 6. Key Market Observations

| Observation | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Only 1 trades-specific provider exists** | Footbridge Media ($199–$249/mo) is the only done-for-you service built specifically for contractors. Massive gap |
| **$99–$249/mo is the budget DFY sweet spot** | $99 Social and Footbridge Media dominate this tier with human-created content |
| **Self-serve tools are cheap but require effort** | Buffer ($5/channel), SocialBee ($29), SocialPilot ($25.50) — affordable but trades business owners won't use them consistently |
| **AI auto-posting is emerging** | Glow Social ($49/mo, 12 AI posts) is the cheapest fully automated option. Quality is the question mark |
| **Premium agencies charge $3,000+/mo for 10 posts** | WebFX charges $300/post effectively. The value is strategy + creative team, not volume |
| **White-label fulfillment is viable** | MixBloom at $149/mo per client for 40 posts = strong margin opportunity for resellers |
| **Video/Reels are almost always upsold** | Short-form video is rarely included in base packages under $500/mo |
| **Google Business Profile posting is underserved** | Many tools and services don't include GBP posts — this is a differentiator for local/trades businesses |
