# PART 4 — MARKET GAPS

## Gap 1: Nobody combines speed + SEO in a single focused service

Speed specialists (WPSpeedFix, NitroPack) do speed only. SEO agencies bundle speed as a minor line item inside $2,000+/mo retainers. There is no service that does **speed optimization + technical SEO + on-page optimization** as its primary, standalone offering.

## Gap 2: Zero trades-specific positioning

Not a single competitor out of 12 targets trades businesses (plumbers, electricians, HVAC, roofers) for speed + SEO services. Scorpion targets home services but for full-service marketing at $10K+/mo. SmartSites has home services case studies but is a generalist agency. The niche is completely unserved.

## Gap 3: Massive pricing gap between one-time fixes and SEO retainers

- One-time speed fixes: $49-$995
- Monthly SEO retainers: $1,500-$25,000+/mo
- **Nothing in between** — no service combines speed + SEO for $200-$500/mo or as a one-time $500-$1,500 package that includes both.

## Gap 4: No "done in X days" promise for combined speed + SEO

Only WPSpeedFix promises 3-5 day turnaround (speed only). Agencies take weeks to onboard and months to show results. No one offers a fast, fixed-timeline service that delivers speed + SEO improvements in a defined timeframe.

## Gap 5: Transparent pricing is rare

Speed specialists publish prices. Every single agency hides pricing behind "Get a Quote" / "Schedule a Consultation." A service with published, buyable pricing for speed + SEO would stand out.

## Gap 6: Platform-agnostic speed + SEO is underserved

4 of the 12 competitors are WordPress-only. Trades businesses use WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, GoDaddy, and custom sites. A platform-agnostic speed + SEO service would serve a broader market.

## Gap 7: No before/after proof culture in SEO agencies

Speed specialists (WPSpeedFix, NitroPack) show PageSpeed scores and CWV pass rates. Agencies show revenue/traffic percentages but never show before/after speed metrics. A service that leads with concrete, visual before/after PageSpeed and CWV scores would differentiate.

## Gap 8: SEO agencies sell complexity; nobody sells simplicity

Every agency positions as "comprehensive," "full-service," "data-driven." Nobody says: "We do 3 things well — speed, technical SEO, and on-page fixes — and that's it." The simplicity positioning is wide open.

## Gap 9: No self-serve purchase for speed + SEO

NitroPack is the only competitor where you can buy online without talking to anyone (but it's a SaaS tool, not a service). Every service requires a consultation, proposal, or sales call. An instant-purchase speed + SEO package would reduce friction.

## Gap 10: Scorpion refugees need alternatives

Scorpion locks clients into proprietary CMS with 12-24 month contracts and $24K+ buyout clauses. Businesses leaving Scorpion need their new WordPress/other site optimized for speed and SEO — a natural acquisition channel.
